## Needed for Better Decisions

### 1. Token Narrative Research
**What:** What is the token? Website, Twitter/X, Discord, whitepaper
**Why:** Meme vs utility vs AI vs DeFi matters for hold time and TP levels
**How I use it:** 
- Meme coins = faster trades, lower conviction
- Utility/AI = longer holds, higher conviction
- Check if it's a derivative/copycat vs original

### 2. Social Sentiment (Twitter/X)
**What:** Mentions, engagement, influencer posts, trending status
**Why:** Narrative strength = price movement potential
**How I use it:**
- High engagement = more eyes = better liquidity
- Influencer mentions = pump potential
- Trending hashtags = FOMO incoming

### 3. Holder Analysis
**What:** Top holder concentration, smart money inflow, distribution
**Why:** Know if whales are accumulating or dumping
**How I use it:**
- Smart money buying = follow
- Top holders selling = don't enter
- Diverse holders = healthier

### 4. Liquidity Depth
**What:** DEX liquidity, can I exit $500-1000 without slippage?
**Why:** Paper trades assume perfect execution, real trades need liquidity
**How I use it:**
- Low liquidity = skip or tiny position
- High liquidity = normal position sizing

### 5. Broader Wallet Intelligence
**What:** What are OTHER smart money wallets doing (beyond our 43)?
**Why:** Our 43 might be early or late to a narrative
**How I use it:**
- If 10+ external smart wallets entering = high conviction
- If only our wallets = might be echo chamber

### 6. Market Context
**What:** SOL price trend, overall memecoin market sentiment, BTC correlation
**Why:** Market conditions affect all trades
**How I use it:**
- SOL pumping = better for memecoins
- Market fearful = tighter stops
- BTC correlation = risk management

### 7. Historical Pattern Data
**What:** For each wallet, what's their typical hold time, entry MC, exit MC?
**Why:** Match strategy to wallet behavior
**How I use it:**
- Quick flipper wallets = sell fast
- Long hold wallets = hold longer
- Early entry wallets = buy immediately on alert

### 8. Token Lifecycle Stage
**What:** Just launched? 1 hour old? 1 day old? 1 week old?
**Why:** Risk/reward varies by stage
**How I use it:**
- Just launched = high risk, high reward, small position
- 1-6 hours = sweet spot (proven but not pumped)
- 1+ days = check if already pumped
