#### **Dogwifhat / WIF (Launched: November 2023)**

- "The hat stays on" became iconic community rallying cry
- Las Vegas Sphere fundraising campaign ($700K raised)
- First major memecoin to cross $3 price threshold
- Binance listing propelled massive liquidity
- Organic community growth without marketing budget

#### **Book of Meme / BOME (Launched: March 10, 2024)**
- **Peak Market Cap:** $1.4B+ within 48 hours
- **Current Status:** ~$800M
- **Initial Surge:** 3,000% in first days
- **Launch Type:** Presale by renowned artist Darkfarms

**What Made It Parabolic:**

- Novel concept: "Permanent storage library for memes" using Arweave/IPFS
- Presale raised 10,131 SOL (~$2M at the time) in 24 hours
- One investor turned 420.69 SOL ($75K) into $19M in 36 hours
- First memecoin backed by established meme artist reputation
- Combined art, culture, and technology narrative
- Pre-sale FOMO was unprecedented - everyone wanted allocation

## 4. COMMON FAILURE PATTERNS

### A. RUG PULL MECHANICS

**The Statistics (Alarming):**
- **98.6%** of Pump.fun tokens show pump-and-dump or rug characteristics
- **93%** of Raydium pools exhibit manipulation signs
- Average drawdown from ATH: **72%** across all memecoins

**Rug Pull Types:**

| Type | How It Works | Detection |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| **Liquidity Rug** | Deployer removes LP tokens | Check if LP is locked/burned |
| **Honeypot** | Contract prevents selling | Test with small amount first |
| **Insider Dump** | Dev wallets sell into pump | Check dev wallet activity |
| **Fee Switch** | Sudden 99% sell tax | Read contract for mutable taxes |
| **Mint Function** | Infinite token creation | Verify mint authority revoked |
| **Hidden Wallets** | Dev uses multiple addresses | Use Bubblemaps to cluster |

### B. RED FLAGS CHECKLIST

**Critical (Avoid Immediately):**
- [ ] Contract has mint function still active
- [ ] Liquidity not locked (can be removed anytime)
- [ ] Top 3 wallets hold >50% of supply
- [ ] Developer wallets actively selling
- [ ] Contract prevents selling (honeypot)
- [ ] Tax can be changed by owner

**Warning (Proceed with Caution):**
- [ ] Anonymous team with no reputation
- [ ] Copycat name/ticker of successful token
- [ ] Artificial volume (bot trading)
- [ ] Fake holder count (multiple wallets, one owner)
- [ ] No social media presence
- [ ] Launched during peak euphoria

**Verification Tools:**
- RugCheck.xyz (Solana-specific)
- Token Sniffer
- Honeypot.is
- Bubblemaps (wallet clustering)
- SolanaFM (contract explorer)
- GMGN (holder analysis)

### C. SLOW BLEED INDICATORS

**Death Spiral Patterns:**
1. **Volume Cliff:** 80%+ volume drop in 48 hours
2. **Holder Stagnation:** No new unique holders for weeks
3. **Community Decay:** Telegram dead, Twitter only bots
4. **Dev Abandonment:** No updates, no engagement
5. **Liquidity Drain:** Gradual LP removal

**Recovery Odds:**
- 70%+ drawdown: 30% chance of recovery
- 90%+ drawdown: 10% chance of recovery
- 95%+ drawdown: <5% chance (effectively dead)
