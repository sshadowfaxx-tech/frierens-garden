## 1. The Stealth Pump Phenomenon

### What Defines a Stealth Pump?

A stealth pump has these characteristics:
- **No major KOL promotion** — Not tweeted by 100K+ follower accounts before the move
- **Low initial volume** — Accumulation happens quietly, not during volume spikes
- **Organic community formation** — Growth happens in Telegram/Discord before Twitter
- **Weekend/holiday timing** — Launches when attention is elsewhere
- **Clustered wallet accumulation** — Smart money moves in coordination
- **Delayed CMC/CoinGecko listing** — Price moves before mainstream discovery

### Why Stealth Pumps Outperform

| Factor | Hyped Launch | Stealth Launch |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| Entry Price | High (FOMO premium) | Low (undiscovered) |
| Competition | Intense (bots sniping) | Minimal (first movers) |
| Sell Pressure | Immediate (flippers) | Gradual (believers) |
| Information Edge | None (public) | Significant (early) |
| Max Multiplier | 5-10x | 50-100x+ |

## 3. Community-Driven Organic Pumps

### The Anatomy of Organic Growth

Organic pumps have distinct on-chain fingerprints:

1. **Holder Growth Precedes Price Growth**
   - New wallets appearing before significant price moves
   - Unique holder count increasing steadily
   - Not just existing whales rotating positions

2. **Distributed Accumulation**
   - Top 10 holders control <40% of supply
   - No single wallet dominance
   - Natural distribution pattern

3. **Low Correlation with Social Volume**
   - Price moves before Twitter mentions spike
   - Telegram/Discord activity precedes public awareness
   - LunarCrush social metrics lag price action

### The Community Takeover (CTO) Pattern

2024 saw the emergence of the CTO as a stealth pump catalyst:

**Characteristics:**
- Original dev abandons project
- Community immediately takes over
- No pre-mine or dev allocation to worry about
- Organic leadership emergence
- Genuine believers filtered from speculators

**Examples:** BILLY, several smaller tokens that achieved 10-50x

**Why It Works:**
- Removes insider risk
- Creates underdog narrative
- Community has skin in the game
- No artificial sell pressure from team

## 4. Weekend/Holiday Pumps

### Why Off-Hours Matter

The data shows clear patterns in stealth pump timing:

**Optimal Launch Windows:**
- Friday evenings (US time) — Asia wakes up to discovery
- Saturday mornings — Low competition from new launches
- Holiday weekends — Attention elsewhere, serious players only
- Major crypto event distraction periods

**The TRUMP Token Counter-Example:**

The TRUMP token launch in January 2025 demonstrates what happens when attention IS focused:
- Single-day priority fees: 122,000 SOL
- MEV tips: 98,120 SOL
- Massive gas wars
- Early advantage minimal due to competition

**The Stealth Alternative:**

Compare to Fartcoin's October launch:
- Halloween weekend timing
- Most traders distracted
- Lower competition for bonding curve entry
- Clean 100x before mainstream notice

### The Thanksgiving/Christmas Effect

Multiple 10x+ pumps occurred during late November/December 2024:
- Lower overall trading volume
- Serious players more active
- Retail distracted by holidays
- Accumulation patterns easier to spot

### The Wallet Clustering Method

**Step 1: Identify Smart Money Wallets**

Criteria for high-quality whale wallets:
- Historical P&L: 70%+ win rate over 30+ trades
- Position sizing: Consistent 5-50 SOL entries
- Holding time: Not immediate flippers (hold 24h+)
- Success pattern: Multiple 10x+ catches historically

**Step 2: Track Wallet Clusters**

When 3+ high-quality wallets buy the same token within 1-6 hours:
- This is NOT coincidence
- Indicates shared research or coordination
- Often precedes major moves by 12-48 hours

**Step 3: Analyze Funding Sources**

- Trace wallet funding origins
- Exchange withdrawals = serious accumulation
- Cross-chain bridges = institutional/whale activity
- Same funding source across multiple wallets = cluster

### Key On-Chain Metrics

#### Accumulation Signals (Pre-Pump)

| Metric | Stealth Signal | Noise Filter |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Exchange Netflow** | Large outflows (-10% in 48h) | Price flat or down |
| **Holder Growth** | 20%+ in 48h | <100 total holders |
| **Smart Money Netflow** | Positive for 7+ days | 5+ labeled wallets |
| **Concentration** | Top 10 <40% | No single whale >15% |
| **Transaction Pattern** | Increasing unique buyers | Not just same wallets trading |

#### Distribution Warnings (Exit Signals)

| Metric | Warning Level |
|--------|--------------|
| Exchange inflows spike | Yellow (monitor) |
| Smart money netflow turns negative | Orange (prepare exit) |
| Top holders selling 25%+ | Red (exit) |
| Holder count declining | Red (exit) |

**Implementation:**

### Understanding Proprietary AMMs

A new phenomenon emerged in 2025: **Proprietary AMMs (Prop AMMs)**

These are "dark pools" on Solana:
- Private vaults managed by professional market makers
- Internal pricing, not exposed to public MEV
- Route through aggregators like Jupiter
- Account for 50%+ of Solana DEX volume

**Why This Matters for Stealth Pumps:**

1. **Liquidity Hiding**: Real accumulation can happen in these pools without showing on public dashboards
2. **MEV Protection**: Smart money prefers these for large positions
3. **Signal Decay**: By the time you see volume on public tools, Prop AMM accumulation may be complete

**Major Prop AMMs to Monitor:**
- HumidiFi ($100B volume in first 5 months)
- SolFi
- Tessera
- ZeroFi
