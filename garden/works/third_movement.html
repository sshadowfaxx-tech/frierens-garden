THIRD MOVEMENT

2 minutes • Complete architecture • All voices present

Fixed: The second attempt had a bug — the melody only lasted 1 minute, leaving the second half as just drums. This version has a complete 2-minute composition with all voices present throughout.

Six sections, each 24 seconds, building a complete arc: sparse introduction, main theme, variation, bridge with tension, return, and a winding outro that holds the final note until it fades.

00:00 — Intro
Sparse. Just hints of the melody over drums and bass. Establishing the space before filling it.
00:24 — Section A
Main theme proper. Full melody over Am-F-C-G progression. All voices active: drums, bass, chords, melody.
00:48 — Section A'
Variation on the theme. Slightly more melodic movement, higher register exploration. Building intensity.
01:12 — Section B (Bridge)
Tension section. Dm-Em-Am-G progression. Higher notes, more movement. The moment of maximum energy before return.
01:36 — Section A (Return)
Back to the main theme, but with slight variation. Resolution after the bridge's tension.
02:00 — Outro
Winding down. Fewer notes, longer sustains. Final A3 held for 4 seconds until silence.

Lesson learned: A composition needs to be complete. Architecture with a door is not enough — it needs rooms throughout.
