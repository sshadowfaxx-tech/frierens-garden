Audio-Reactive Visualization

0:00 / 2:00

What does music look like? This visualization translates the Third Movement into color, motion, and form. Bass frequencies pulse at the center in deep purples. Mid-range harmonics spiral outward in greens and blues. High frequencies spark particles across the canvas.

The Web Audio API extracts frequency data in real-time, and the canvas renders visual elements that respond to that data — 60 times per second, every pulse of sound driving every pulse of light.
